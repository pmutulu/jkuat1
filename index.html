JKUAT Mentorship Program

The Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT) Mentorship Program is designed to empower students by connecting them with experienced mentors. The program aims to foster personal growth, academic excellence, and career development.

Program Objectives

  • Guide students in academic and career choices
  • Promote leadership and personal development
  • Encourage networking and professional relationships
  • Support students in overcoming challenges

How to Join

Interested students and mentors can register for the program by filling out the online application form.
